About Us

Welcome to
KADOKAWA GEMPAK STARZ

We are the foremost comic publishing company in Malaysia. Learn about who we are, what we have done, our mission, and our vision in the world of publishing and beyond!

Creating original content that not only encourages and uplifts people but informs and entertains them is at the heart of what we do and is a task we take very seriously.

AIR TEAM

AIR TEAM’s promise is to give their readers the best comics!

BLACK INK

The goal of the BLACK INK team is to rouse hope and courage in their readers via the simple, basic medium of black ink used by their pens and brushes.

CANDY FACTORY

Their focus is to provide their readers with a different and delightful visual experience.

SOLAR TEAM

Harnessing the power and energy of the Sun itself, ‘Solar’ combines next-gen sophistication with a symbol of permanence, ensuring that readers will not forget the bright new creative force rising in the industry.

DREAMERZ

Dreamerz coalesced out of the duo’s shared belief that enthusiasm is key in sustaining the pursuit of one’s dreams.
